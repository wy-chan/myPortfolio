Fairy in the Forest

This picture is about a fairy in the forest. The wings are inspired by insect wings and animal veins. She has thin wings and dress that light can pass through. The light helps focus the attention to the fairy comparing with the darker background.


Software used: Photoshop (brush tool)

Winter Mermaid

Mermaids always leave a beautiful and elegant impression, but in this scene, I would like to create a mermaid that also looks strong and tough.

Piano

It is a butterfly-inspired grand piano with a lid in the shape of a butterfly wing and the wooden body with butterfly-shaped carves. The main objects are dim because I would like to create a scent of mystery to this picture.


Software used: Photoshop (brush tool)

Original Characters

Pink Dolphin Camellia

Camellia is a character inspired by Indo-Pacific humpback dolphins. They are pink dolphins that live in the coastal waters of the Indian and western Pacific oceans. Camellia has an iconic dophin-shaped cap, a dophin-tail-shaped hair style and fin-shaped ears. She likes swimming freely in the ocean.


Software used: Photoshop (brush tool)

Mr. Pillow & Tini

Tini is a little girl who is always sleepy. Mr. Pillow was Tini's pillow originally, but one day it magically came to live when Tini drawn his face on the pillow. They became friends and have exciting adventures in the dreams everynight.


Software used: Photoshop (brush tool)

Red Panda Mochi

Mochi is a cute red panda with a round body. Although Mochi is lazy, he is enjoying his life everyday. His favourite activities are eating bamboo leaves and sleeping. Mochi is slightly fatter than other red pandas, but he can climb the trees quickly and find foods efficiently.


Software used: Photoshop (brush tool)
